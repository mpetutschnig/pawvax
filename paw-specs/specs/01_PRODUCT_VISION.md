# 01 - Product Vision: PAW

## Core Purpose
PAW (Digitaler Tierimpfpass) is a secure, AI-powered platform designed to replace physical pet health records. It bridges the gap between pet owners, veterinarians, and authorities by providing a verifiable, digital-first health identity for animals.

## High-Level Value Proposition
1. **For Owners**: Effortless digitization of paper records via AI, reminders for vaccinations, and easy sharing (QR/NFC).
2. **For Veterinarians**: Digital verification of records, streamlined document entry, and "Vet-Verified" status for trust.
3. **For Authorities**: Fast, mobile-first access to vaccination status (e.g., Rabies) during checks via public scan pages.

## Key Differentiators
- **AI-First**: Uses Gemini Vision 1.5 Flash-Lite to extract structured data from messy, handwritten or stamped medical documents.
- **Privacy-Preserving Sharing**: Fine-grained control over what data is shared during a scan.
- **Auditability**: Every change is logged; sensitive health data is immutable once verified by a professional.
- **PWA Excellence**: Fully functional on mobile without the friction of an app store, utilizing NFC and offline capabilities.
